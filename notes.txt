This should help to compile the libraries on windows:
https://pracucci.com/compile-ffmpeg-on-windows-with-visual-studio-compiler.html

According to @tobiashieta (conan-slack) when you compile FFmpeg with MSVC you cannot get
optimizations in ASM, etc. It is better to cross-compile.

https://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/CompilationGuide/CrossCompilingForWindows
http://conanio.readthedocs.io/en/latest/howtos/cross_building.html?highlight=cross

$ ./configure --arch=x86 --target-os=mingw32 --cross-prefix=i686-w64-mingw32- --pkg-config=pkg-config
--arch= x86 x86_32 x86_64
--target-os= mingw32*|mingw64*|win32|win64|cygwin*|*-dos|freedos|opendos|os/2*|symbian)
--enable-cross-compile
